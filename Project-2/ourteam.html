Our employees are the secret to our success. Company-wide, across all of the Hamilton Divisions, every employee is given the support and autonomy required to do their job safely, efficiently, and effectively. Our employees are talented, passionate, and dedicated. Many of them have celebrated 10 or more years with us and love what they do. As a result, we are a hands-on contractor recognized for our skilled workforce and our commitment to excellence through teamwork.

Hamilton has a long history of being committed to a diverse workforce. Our strategy is simple: treat people with respect, be fair and honest, create a level playing field, provide a safe worksite, and offer a supportive environment. Our effort to diversify our employee workforce and project partners is reflected in our project bidding, recruitment, apprenticeship, and retention strategies.

We are a privately held company, providing opportunities for career advancement, competitive compensation, and a work environment that is second to none. Hamilton provides comprehensive safety training and benefit programs to ensure our culture of health and safety is possible on and off the job. We do amazing work in beautiful locations and we want our team and their families to enjoy every aspect of working for Hamilton.
